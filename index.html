TWITCH

UX DESIGN // FUTURE VISION // TEAM’S PROJECT

Step into a imagination streaming experience where the elevation of the content to new heights.

In the UX Foundations class at Keyframe Multimedia School, students were organized into teams of five to collaborate on a project focused on redesigning a website and developing a fresh information architecture.

My group choose to work with Twitch, a platform for live video streaming, offering nearly anything you’d want to watch, from cooking, music, Q&A sessions, and — the leading driver of traffic — video games. Twitch is available for streaming on many platforms. You can visit the official Twitch website to watch streams, or use the app, which is available on iOS, Android, Windows, Mac, PS4, PS5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S, and devices like Chromecast and Apple TV. If your device has access to the internet and supports apps, you can probably use it for Twitch.

Problems

With such a vast array of streams available on Twitch, users might find it challenging to discover new content that aligns with their interests. The recommendation algorithms may not always accurately suggest content that users would enjoy.

The recommended solution is to highlight trending and popular streams on the platform to showcase content that is currently gaining traction or receiving high engagement from the community. This could help users discover new and popular content without having to rely solely on the recommendation algorithm.

Solutions

Preview Thumbnails and Descriptions

Improve the visibility and clarity of preview thumbnails and stream descriptions to give users a better idea of what to expect from a stream before clicking on it. This can help users make more informed decisions about which streams to watch. Also need to ntroduce features that allow users to receive recommendations from their friends or other members of the Twitch community. This could include features such as sharing favorite streams, creating watchlists, or participating in community-curated playlists. Another option is to offer follow recommendations based on users' existing follow list, similar to social media platforms. By suggesting other streamers or channels that are similar to ones users already follow, it can help expand their content discovery horizons.
