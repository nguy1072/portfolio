ABOUT ME
My name is Lyn Nguyen , and I am currently pursuing a degree in Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College in Ottawa, Canada.

As a dedicated design student, I have improved my skills through various projects and opportunities. This has involved designing graphics and websites, and helping create engaging user experiences. These experiences have not only deepened my appreciation for design but also allowed me to make a positive impact in my community.

While my primary focus is on design, I also have a deep appreciation for other creative outlets, including photography, filming, and experimenting with various arts and crafts projects.

As I approach graduation, I am eager to pursue opportunities in the field of interactive media design, where I can continue to grow and apply my skills in creating engaging and functional digital experiences.
